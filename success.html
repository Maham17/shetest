Blossom Jewels

CEO: Maham Kashif

"I am the founder of Blossom Jewels, a jewelry brand that specializes in creating trendy and minimalist jewelry for girls. As a young entrepreneur, I started this business with a passion for creating unique pieces that would appeal to a wide audience. Building this business from scratch was not an easy task. I faced numerous challenges along the way, but I was determined to turn my dream into a reality. With hard work and perseverance, I created a stunning collection of jewelry that reflected my unique style and personality."

Labsstitch

CEO: Laiba Querishi

"LabsStitch is my hand-embroidered t-shirt brand that I started to showcase my love for creative designs and personal style. As an avid fan of unique and trendy fashion, I wanted to create something that would stand out and make a statement. That's how I came up with the idea of hand embroidering t-shirts with my own designs. Even though there were challenges along the way, I kept going and never lost sight of my vision. Today, LabsStitch is a thriving brand that has gained a loyal following of customers who appreciate the uniqueness and quality of my designs."

Zuha Captures

"I'm a photographer with a passion for capturing the beauty of nature and the world around us. From breathtaking landscapes to delicate flowers, I love exploring the natural world through my lens and sharing my vision with others.
